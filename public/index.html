Our Mission

Our mission is to digitize every aspect of medical interactions and make them accessible to everyone in a safe and secure manner, without any cost.

MedRec is a division of Ignite Research that was founded in April 2020 with the objective of accelerating global communication through digital means. At MedRec, we strive to comprehend the requirements of every healthcare professional and create software that is convenient for both patients and doctors to use. Our mission is to overcome the limitations of geography and language, connect patients with healthcare professionals, and expedite access to high-quality medical treatment, without any cost.
